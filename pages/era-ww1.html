The Legacy of WWI

WW1 killed the old European order. Four empires — the Ottoman, Austro-Hungarian, German, and Russian — dissolved. The map of the Middle East was redrawn by British and French diplomats with little regard for the peoples involved, creating borders that still cause conflict today. The Versailles Treaty's humiliation of Germany created the economic and political conditions that brought Hitler to power. WW1 didn't just end — it detonated.
